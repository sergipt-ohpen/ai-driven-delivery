## 🧠 On AI-assisted planning in general

## 🎯 On estimation with AI (papers and technique)

-   📖 [arXiv 2603.06276 — Story Point Estimation Using LLMs (Mar 2026)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06276) · The reference paper. Technical but readable. Key conclusion: LLMs achieve accuracy comparable to project-specific deep learning models without project training data.
    
-   📖 [arXiv 2505.16290 — Multimodal Generative AI for SP Estimation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.16290) · Multimodal (text + images + categoricals). Useful for contexts where stories include mockups.
    
-   📖 [Springer — Improving SP estimation with ensemble ML](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11219-025-09731-6) · Systematic literature review. Useful for understanding the state of the art.
    
-   📖 [Agile Leadership Day India — How to do Sprint Planning for AI Agents](https://agileleadershipdayindia.org/blogs/ai-augmented-scrum-framework/ai-augmented-sprint-planning.html) · March 2026. Argues why SPs don't apply to agents and how to assign "compute budgets" instead. Provocative, worth reading.
