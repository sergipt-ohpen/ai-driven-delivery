# 💬 Expectations — 9 min | AI-Driven Delivery

⏳ Estimated time: 6 min 

Read this message carefully. It defines how the program works and what you can realistically expect from this experience.

---

### ⚠ Before we begin: something important

During the first few sessions, you may feel like you are not learning anything new.

This is normal.

The first weeks are designed to align the level of the group and establish a common working foundation. If you already have experience, this block will help you better structure the way you work and consolidate a methodology that you will apply throughout the entire program.

That feeling fades as you move forward.

It does not mean the program is not for you.

#### 👉 What can you expect from us during the program?

-   **Teaching by example.** We want you to achieve excellence, and we will demonstrate that level in every action and decision.
    
-   **Teaching you how to make decisions.** We are not here to give you step-by-step instructions on what to do in every situation, since every project and company is a world of its own. Instead, you will be exposed to new situations and discover different approaches to tackle them (not only from the mentors, but also from your peers). We want you to develop the judgment to choose the right tool for each context, and when and how to use it.
    
-   **8x5 availability (and beyond — don't worry, we live here!).** We are in the course WhatsApp group and also available privately. Whatever your question or difficulty, we are now your team. You can count on us for anything personal or related to AI4Devs, and on the Lidr Support Team for any technical issues.
    
-   **Presenting you with real day-to-day situations from software teams.** We will focus on common problems and dynamics in tech companies.
    
-   **Punctuality in every live session.** We will meet on Zoom at the scheduled time and will be connected 5 minutes early to start on time.
    
-   **A variety of session formats.** We will adapt each session to the situation: workshops, open debates, collaborative work in breakout rooms, Q&A rounds, or informal conversations.
    
-   **Requesting continuous feedback.** Constant improvement is key to excellence, and we adapt the program to your needs based on your comments.
    
-   **Improving quickly based on your input.** We will do everything possible to help you achieve your goals.
    

#### 👉 **What we expect from you:**

-   **Unlearn what you think a training program is.** This is not a traditional course. It is a project in which you develop yourself, and the other participants are not just students, but your team. Your individual success depends on the success of the group. This is the key mindset.
    
-   **Behave** at all times in accordance with the [AI4Devs General Terms and Conditions](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1nLZRMyqufxD5J3eYrEY-3kHYB00-Kjdr/view?usp=sharing) and with our community's [Code of Conduct](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1BYCDDKQenvKVDwkn0JbQEjLxre2F9UHt/view?usp=sharing).
    
-   **Prepare technically before each session.** The main tools will be MightyNetworks, Zoom, and Drive, but we will also use GitHub, Miro, Airtable, and others. Make sure you have a good internet connection, good sound, good lighting, and a distraction-free environment. **Keeping your camera on at all times is mandatory.**
    
-   **Arrive prepared for each live session.** To make the most of our live time together, we will assume a minimum level of preparation. This means having consumed 100% of the mandatory content (videos, documentation) and having worked on the exercises. The more time you invest, the better the results.
    
-   **Bring questions and doubts to the live sessions.** With the *flipped-classroom* approach, you are the ones who set the pace. We will focus on the aspects you find most relevant.
    
-   **Ask questions in public, not in private.** We are not the only ones available to help you; your team, a group of great international talents, is also here to support you. In addition, you will surface questions that others may have, enriching everyone's experience.
    
-   **Prepare proposals and solutions to exercises in whatever format you prefer.** Document, presentation, video... although we especially recommend **GitHub and Notion.**
    
-   **Demonstrate proactivity, initiative, curiosity, critical thinking, and a team attitude.** We expect *senior* behavior in all your actions. Take it seriously: these are essential elements to make the most of this experience.
    
-   **Give constructive and actionable feedback.** The key to improvement is contributing solutions, not just pointing out problems. If something isn't working, tell us what and suggest a viable improvement.
    
-   **Exercise review.** To ensure a fair and agile process, we will select a random sample of 10 submitted exercises for review and correction. Based on this, we will make general observations that will help the whole group improve. If your exercise is not selected, don't worry: you can benefit from the observations and apply the lessons learned in your next submissions.
    

---

📍 **This last point is fundamental.** You will have different ways to share your feedback with us:

1⃣ At the end of each live session, a ROTI (Return On Time Invested) survey will be available on the platform, 15 minutes before the session ends.

2⃣ At the end of each module, you will have an optional survey to evaluate the content and the exercise of that module.

3⃣ In addition, in the "Useful Links" section, you have a form to leave more specific and detailed feedback, either anonymously or with your name, whichever you prefer.

**Remember**: we cannot promise to implement every suggestion, but we will evaluate 100% of them.

Finally, **enjoy the process!**

**As you can see, both sides must make a conscious effort for AI4Devs to be a success.**
