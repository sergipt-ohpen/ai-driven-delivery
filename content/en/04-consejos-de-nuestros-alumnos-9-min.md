## Ximena Paparella - VP of Engineering

**Recommendations**

-   Focus on understanding the **common thread of the program**, not on consuming isolated content. That is where learning really happens.
    
-   Take advantage of the master's program to understand the **complete AI development cycle**, not just loose pieces. The holistic view is what makes the difference.
    
-   Prioritize learning with **structure and method**, especially in a context where there is an excess of information and tools.
    
-   Put every concept into practice so you can apply it to real projects and get tangible results.
    
-   Don't scatter yourself constantly chasing new tools: focus on the working system the program proposes.
