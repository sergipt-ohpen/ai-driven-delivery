**Are you ready?** 💪

AI4Devs is a live online program that prepares you to harness the potential of code assistants and other AI-powered tools in your day-to-day work. It doesn't just teach you how to use these tools; you will also improve your knowledge of software engineering best practices, essential soft skills in this new context, and above all your mindset and attitude toward the changes coming to the industry.

AI4Devs 🤖 is a 20-week program divided into 2-hour group sessions (38 in total), a 30-hour individual project, and more than 10 hours of recorded content and additional material. It will require a minimum of 4 and an average of 6 hours of work per week.
